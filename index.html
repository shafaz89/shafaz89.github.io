Staphylococcal cell biology • Cell Division • Envelope Biosynthesis • Protein Glycosylation

My research explores staphylococcal cell biology, with a focus on the roles of bacterial spectrins in cell division, membrane organization, and surface protein glycosylation. In addition, we investigate the accessory Sec (aSec) pathway to understand how its components glycosylate surface proteins and how these modifications influence staphylococcal physiology and virulence.
